Bio

My name is Chandler Bullock and I am a recent graduate from the University of Calgary. I earned my Bsc. in Computer Science in 2020. There I experimented with human computer interaction, and also worked a lot with databases. I completed projects utilizing machine learning, created user applications with complicated back-end systems, and experimented with visualizing large data sets.

Experience

I've been working as an intern at Eidetic Communications, a small startup, for the last year. We develop compute accelerators to drive cost savings and performance gains for large data centers. At Eideticom I've been able to develop an array of visualization and reporting tools to give management and other developers better visibility of our product's performance. I've also been instrumental in creating and managing continuous integration pipelines for our core repositories.

Goals

My career goals are to expand my understanding of web technologies, and sharpen my design skills. I feel confident with my experience that I can master any programming language or technical tool. With that as my foundation I'm looking to dive deeper into the visual design and user experience problem space. I have found exploring the field of design and bridging it with my technical expertise to be extremely gratifying. I hope to one day have a comprehensive understanding of what makes digital products successful.
